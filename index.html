Who We Are

Specialist NDIS Support for Complex Needs

At ThriveAbility Care, we support National Disability Insurance Scheme (NDIS) participants with high-intensity and complex care needs through flexible, tailored services that respect your routine, goals and preferences.

Whether you need help with personal care, daily living, community participation or in-home support, we deliver care that is consistent, safe and focused on your wellbeing. Support can be one-on-one or provided by multiple support workers when higher support ratios are required.

Support that works with your life, delivered your way.
How We Support You

Our Core NDIS Services

Social & Community Participation

We support you to stay active and connected in your local community in ways that suit you. Whether it’s heading to the shops, visiting a community centre, joining a social group or enjoying a swim at the beach, we’re here to make it happen.

Learn more

Short-Term Accommodation

Looking for a break or a change of scenery? Our short-term accommodation offers a safe, comfortable space where you can relax, enjoy new experiences and build independence with the right support around you.

Learn more

Assistance With Daily Living

We provide hands-on support to help you manage daily tasks and live as independently as possible. From personal care and meal preparation to home support and routines, we’re here to make everyday living easier and more empowering.

Learn more

Day Programs

Our structured day programs are all about connection, confidence and fun. Join in on meaningful activities that help you learn new skills, meet others and enjoy your day in a welcoming, supportive environment.

Why ThriveAbility

Trusted by participants, families and support coordinators across New South Wales

Partnering with ThriveAbility Care means working with a team that genuinely cares about your goals, your wellbeing and your quality of life. We don’t just deliver NDIS supports. We take the time to understand what matters most to you, then shape our services around it to ensure you receive all the support you need.

Our experienced support staff provide consistent, reliable care that meets NDIS standards while staying flexible to your changing needs. We focus on building real relationships, not just ticking boxes.

Whether you need help at home, in the community or with high-intensity support, we are here to make things easier, safer and more empowering at every stage.

Choose ThriveAbility Care and move forward with a provider who is committed to your growth, your independence and your right to thrive.
