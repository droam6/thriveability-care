Our Services

NDIS services tailored to you

At ThriveAbility Care, we deliver personalised NDIS services designed for individuals with high-intensity needs. Our focus is on creating dependable, person-centred support that empowers each participant to lead a more independent and fulfilling life.

Our Approach

How we support you

Tailored NDIS Support

Every person’s goals and daily routines are different. We take time to listen, understand and collaborate with you and your support network to deliver services that reflect your needs and preferences.

Complex and High-Intensity Support

Our experienced team specialises in working with participants who require complex care. We provide reliable, skilled support that ensures safety, comfort and consistency, no matter how intensive your needs.

Comprehensive Care Planning

We don’t just deliver services, we help you make sense of them. With a focus on practical supports and smart coordination, we walk alongside you to ensure services remain relevant, flexible and outcome-focused.

Empowering You at Every Step

We’re more than a provider, we’re your partners in progress. From daily living to social connection, our team is committed to helping you build confidence, grow capacity and take control of your path forward.

Our Core NDIS Services

Choose the support that fits your plan

Social & Community Participation

Be part of your world, your way.

At ThriveAbility Care, we believe in the value of shared experiences and connection. We’re here to help you:

  • Build confidence in social settings
  • Strengthen communication and relationship skills
  • Explore hobbies, interests and recreational activities
  • Navigate new environments safely and comfortably
  • Develop a sense of purpose and belonging

From one-on-one outings to small group support, we tailor the experience to your comfort level and goals. Participation isn’t one-size-fits-all, it’s about helping you feel part of something that matters to you.

Short-Term Accommodation

Supportive stays when you need a break.

Our Short-Term Accommodation services give you the chance to experience new environments in a safe and welcoming setting. Whether you need a short stay due to carer respite, an emergency situation or a desire to change up your routine, we’re here to provide a home away from home. What we offer:

  • Comfortable, accessible and well-maintained spaces
  • 24/7 support from trained, friendly staff
  • Personalised care based on your daily routine and preferences
  • Engaging in-house or community-based activities
  • Flexible stays from overnight to multiple days, depending on your needs

We understand the importance of familiarity and trust, especially when staying somewhere new. That’s why we take time to get to know you, communicate clearly and create an experience that’s as calm, fun and supportive as possible.

Learn more

Assistance With Daily Living

Helping you live independently and with dignity.

We provide hands-on support for everyday tasks that help you maintain your independence and enjoy your day-to-day life. Whether you need help for a few hours a day or more consistent support, our team works with you to build routines that suit your lifestyle and preferences. Our daily living supports can include:

  • Personal care (showering, grooming, toileting)
  • Meal planning, preparation and feeding assistance
  • Household tasks (laundry, tidying, cleaning)
  • Medication prompts and health routines
  • Mobility and physical support
  • Transportation and errands

Every plan is tailored to what matters most to you. We listen closely, encourage your involvement and promote self-determination wherever possible, always respecting your pace and preferences.

Learn more

Day Programs

Purposeful days, empowering experiences.

Our Day Programs are designed to enrich lives through structured activities, social interaction, and hands-on learning. We provide inclusive, welcoming spaces where participants can explore interests, build confidence, and enjoy routines outside the home. Each program is tailored to individual goals, with support staff who ensure every activity is engaging and accessible. Activities include:

  • Arts and crafts, music, cooking, and gardening
  • Group outings and community participation
  • Health and wellbeing sessions
  • Life skill workshops and capacity building

We also collaborate with allied health professionals to align activities with broader developmental goals. Whether attending regularly or casually, participants are supported to grow, connect, and thrive in a way that suits their lifestyle.
