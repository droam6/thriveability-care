Assistance With Daily Living

Supporting independence with dignity and care

At ThriveAbility Care, we believe that everyone deserves the opportunity to live with comfort, independence and confidence. Our Assistance with Daily Living services are designed to support NDIS participants with everyday tasks, helping you maintain control over your daily routine while living safely and comfortably at home.

Whether you need low-touch support or high-intensity care, our team is equipped to deliver respectful, professional assistance tailored to your needs and preferences.

What We Help With

Hands-on support across every part of your day

Personal Care

  • Showering, bathing and hygiene routines
  • Dressing and grooming
  • Assistance with eating, drinking and mealtime support

Household Support

  • Light cleaning, tidying and sanitising
  • Meal preparation and cooking
  • Laundry, ironing and home organisation

Mobility and Access

  • Help moving safely around the home
  • Assistance with transfers (bed, chair, toilet)
  • Transport to appointments, errands or activities

High-Intensity Daily Support

Our team is qualified to provide complex support for participants with higher care needs. This includes:

  • Complex bowel care
  • Tracheostomy support
  • Ventilator care and monitoring
  • Medication administration (including injections)
  • Mealtime management for participants with swallowing risks
  • Seizure management, PEG feeding, and other specialised care tasks
Why It Matters

More than help. A foundation for a fuller life.

Our daily living supports are more than just practical help. They are designed to:

  • Foster independence and build daily living skills
  • Promote dignity and respect in all aspects of care
  • Boost confidence and comfort at home and in the community
  • Ensure safety and quality of life through reliable, tailored assistance
Who It’s For

Designed around your NDIS plan

This service is suitable for NDIS participants with funding under Assistance with Daily Living or Core Supports, including those with high-intensity needs. We work closely with participants, families, and support coordinators to ensure your care plan aligns with your goals and lifestyle.
