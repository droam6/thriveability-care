Social & Community Participation

Building confidence, connection and community

At ThriveAbility Care, Social and Community Participation is more than just attending activities. It is about creating meaningful opportunities for connection, skill-building and personal growth. We believe that everyone deserves the chance to engage with their community, develop lasting relationships and enjoy a fulfilling social life on their own terms.

What We Offer

Real participation, on your terms

We provide tailored, flexible supports that help NDIS participants take part in the community in ways that suit their interests, goals and comfort level. Our experienced support workers are there to encourage, assist and empower each individual to take part in the activities that matter to them. Our supports can include:

  • Attending local events, markets or cultural festivals
  • Visiting parks, cafés, libraries, and public spaces
  • Joining hobby groups, sports clubs or creative workshops
  • Volunteering with community organisations
  • Going to the beach or for a swim, exploring nature or heading to the shops
  • Building social skills through one-on-one or small group interactions

We aim to create moments of joy, confidence and connection every step of the way. Whether it’s your first time joining a group or you’re ready to try something new, we’re here to support you in making the most of each experience.

Why It Matters

Connection changes lives

Social and community participation is linked to improved wellbeing, confidence and independence. Our services are designed to reduce isolation and build practical life skills in real-world settings. With the right support, participants can create lasting friendships, gain a sense of belonging, and contribute meaningfully to their communities.

How to Get Started

Using your NDIS plan

If your NDIS plan includes funding under Social and Community Participation or Core Supports, you can use it to access our services. Not sure where to start? Our team will walk you through your options, explain how your plan can be used, and work with you to build a schedule that suits your lifestyle and preferences.

Ready to take the next step?

Speak with our team today and discover how we can support you to thrive.
