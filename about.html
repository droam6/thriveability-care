About ThriveAbility Care

More than a provider. A partner in your journey.

At ThriveAbility Care, we are not here to simply deliver services. We are here to walk alongside you with care, consistency and a focus on what truly matters. As a registered NDIS provider based in New South Wales, we specialise in high-intensity and complex care while keeping people and relationships at the centre.

Who We Are

Built around people, not paperwork

Our Mission

As an up-and-coming registered NDIS service provider in Australia, ThriveAbility Care is driven by a genuine passion for making a meaningful difference in the lives of those we serve. Our team is composed of dedicated professionals who have chosen this path out of love for the work, not just as a career. With diverse backgrounds and a shared commitment to inclusivity, we welcome everyone into our community.

Our Commitment

Empowering Independence — We support you to build confidence, learn new skills and live on your terms.

Delivering Quality Support Every Day — We specialise in services for high-intensity NDIS participants. You can rely on us for support that is consistent, professional and tailored to your needs.

Forming Genuine Relationships — We work in partnership with you, your family and your support team to create strong connections built on trust, respect and clear communication.

Why ThriveAbility Care

We are a growing and registered provider with a strong purpose. Our focus is on delivering care that is personalised, consistent and grounded in real understanding. We value people more than process. Outcomes matter to us, and so does the experience along the way. Whether you need assistance at home, in the community or with daily tasks, we are here to make life easier and more empowering.
